S/5_12-18_T4 Задание 2. Проверочные работы к уроку 12-18 (уч. И.Н. Верещагиной)

Найди ошибки и напиши правильное предложение


1. 

I think you shouldn't make pancakes today.

(1 point)

I don't think you should make pancakes today.


2. 

Why is the dog look hungry?

You have given it some meat, haven't you?

(1 point)

Why does the dog look hungry?


3. 

She said that Tom is at the party.

(1 point)

She said that Tom was at the party.


4. 

Neither Margaret nor her sister has cooked nothing for dinner.

(1 point)

Either Margaret or her sister has cooked nothing for dinner.


5. 

The Johnsons said that they would move to their new house in a week.

(1 point)

The Johnsons said that they would move to their new house a week later.


6. 

Ted asks are they from Africa.

(1 point)

Ted asks they are from Africa.


7. 

Nobody likes when it rains.

(1 point)

Nobody likes it when it rains.


8. 

Christopher was able to get a very useful information from the Internet.

(1 point)

Christopher was able to get very useful information from the Internet.


9. 

The girls's mother said that she would have to work every Saturday.

(1 point)

The girls' mother said that she would have to work every Saturday.


10. 

They didn't like the film. - So did I.

(1 point)

- Neither did I.
