Найди ошибки и напиши правильное предложение


1. 

She cooks bad.

(1 point)

She cooks badly.


2.

(1 point)

Who is writing a test now?


3.

(1 point)

Have you got a sister?


4.

(1 point)

My friends want to be policemen.


5.

(1 point)

There is some ice cream in the box, isn't there?


6.

(1 point)

7.

(1 point)

Does it often snow in winter?


8.

- I don't know. I didn't take it.


9.
