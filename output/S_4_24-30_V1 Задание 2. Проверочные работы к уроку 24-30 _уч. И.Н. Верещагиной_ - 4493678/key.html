S/4_24-30_V1 Задание 2. Проверочные работы к уроку 24-30 (уч. И.Н. Верещагиной)

Найди ошибки и напиши правильное предложение


1. 

Much people celebrate Christmas on the 7th of January.

(1 point)

Many people celebrate Christmas on the 7th of January.


2. 

It was so dark. He could see the houses hard.

(1 point)

It was so dark. He could see the houses hardly.


3. 

He wants to go to London. I think he will happy there.

(1 point)

He wants to go to London. I think he will be happy there.


4. 

My mother never was in London.

(1 point)

My mother has never been to London.


5. 

Who did lock the door?

(1 point)

Who locked the door?


6. 

- Will you wash the floor, please?

- But I have wash the floor.

(1 point)

- But I have washed the floor.


7. 

Will be there any sandwiches for us?

(1 point)

Will there be any sandwiches for us?


8. 

How long it takes you to do your homework?

(1 point)

How long does it take you to do your homework?


9. 

There will a new blackboard in our classroom next month.

(1 point)

There will be a new blackboard in our classroom next month.


10. 

We have been to Moscow two years ago.

(1 point)

We were in Moscow two years ago.
