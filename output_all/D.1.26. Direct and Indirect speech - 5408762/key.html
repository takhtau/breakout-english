D.1.26. Direct and Indirect speech

Report the following requests and orders.


1. 

The teacher, "Don't be late, Peter."

(1 point)

The teacher asked Peter not to be late.


2. 

The guard, "Turn back."

(1 point)

The guard ordered to turn back.


3. 

George, "Don't speak over the phone when I'm listening to music, Betty."

(1 point)

George asked Betty not to speak over the phone when he was listening to music.


4. 

The guide, "Mind the step."

(1 point)

The guide asked to mind the step.


5. 

Mother, "Don't eat so many cakes, children."

(1 point)

Mother asked children not to eat so many cakes.


6. 

Peter, "Wait for me, Ann."

(1 point)

Peter asked Ann to wait for him.


7. 

The teacher, "Don't forget to take your textbook, Jim."

(1 point)

The teacher asked Jim not to forget to take his textbook.


8. 

Mum, "Remember to post the letter, Kate."

(1 point)

Mum asked Kate to remember to post the letter.


9. 

Sue, "Don't leave the door open."

(1 point)

Sue asked not to leave the door open.


10. 

The officer, "Open the fire."

(1 point)

The officer ordered to open the fire.
