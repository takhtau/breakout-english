DE_5_Past simple or continuous.

Напиши правильную форму глагола, используя слова в скобках


1. 

Where__________(you/go) when I saw you on the bus last night?

(1 point)

were you going


2. 

_____________(you/enjoy) the film?

(1 point)

Did you enjoy


3. 

When we shared a room, Zoe___________(always/take) my things. It was so annoying!

(1 point)

was always taking


4. 

When I went to get the tickets, I realised I__________(not/have) any money. 

(1 point)

did not have


5. 

When I was young, we__________(go) to France every year on holiday.

(1 point)

went


6. 

Elvis___________(become) famous for the song Blue Suede Shoes.

(1 point)

became


7. 

When you rang last night I_____________(work) in the garden so I didn't hear the phone.

(1 point)

was working


8. 

I_______________(hear) from Davina last night. She says hello.

(1 point)

heard


9. 

The old man_____________(appear) to be very tired and he slowly sat down.

(1 point)

appeared


10. 

We_____________(throw) a surprise party from my brother last Saturday.

(1 point)

threw
