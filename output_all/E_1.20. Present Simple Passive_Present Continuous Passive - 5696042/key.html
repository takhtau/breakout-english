E_1.20. Present Simple Passive/Present Continuous Passive

Преобразуй слова в скобках так, чтобы они верно дополнили предложение


1. 

Papers (deliver) usually at 8 in the morning, they (look through) at the moment and you’ll get yours soon.

(1 point)

are usually delivered/are being looked through


2. 

Dresses (make) preferably of cotton in hot countries. This wonderful costume (make) specially for this performance now.

(1 point)

are made/is being made


3. 

What strange sounds! – Oh, our piano (tune).

(1 point)

is being tuned


4. 

Where is your car? – It (fill) in the garage at the moment.

(1 point)

is being filled


5. 

Tea (lay) usually on the balcony in fine weather.

(1 point)

is usually laid


6. 

We are finishing the last preparations for the party: the lights (switch on), the floors (clean), the tables (lay). Do you think we’ll be ready on time?

(1 point)

are being switched on/are being cleaned/are being laid


7. 

I’ve got two questions to you. First: “What language (speak) all over the world?” Second: “What language (speak) in this room?”

(1 point)

is spoken/is being spoken


8. 

The witness (question) by the police-inspector now.

(1 point)

is being questioned


9. 

The old motorway (use) by many people, but it’s not very convenient, that’s why a new ring-road (build) in the city.

(1 point)

is used/is being built


10. 

Our luggage (examine) at the Customs now. Any luggage going abroad (check) usually here.

(1 point)

is being examined/is usually checked
