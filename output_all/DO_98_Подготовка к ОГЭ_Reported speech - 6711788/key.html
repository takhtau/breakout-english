DO_98_Подготовка к ОГЭ_Reported speech

Перепишите предложения в косвенной речи, используя выделенные слова


1. 

'I'm sorry I told everyone your secret,' my sister said. (apologised) 

(1 point)

My sister apologised for telling everyone my secret.


2. 

"I didn't give the money to John,' said Ali. (denied) 

(1 point)

Ali denied giving the money to John.


3. 

'I'll love you forever!' Francis said to Elizabeth. (promised) 

(1 point)

Francis promised to love Elizabeth.


4. 

'No, I won't open the door!' said Mandy. (refused) 

(1 point)

Mandy refused to open the door.


5. 

'Why don't we give Jenny a call?' Albert said. (suggested) 

(1 point)

Albert suggested giving Jenny a call.
