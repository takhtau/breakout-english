D_8.3 Pronouns

Fill in the blanks with the appropriate pronouns. (my/our/it/his/her/their/its)


1. 

Jill and Jack write articles for _____ school newspaper.

(1 point)

their


2. 

Bob nodded at _____ wife as if he wanted to say "You see?".

(1 point)

his


3. 

Do you think they are losing _____ popularity?

(1 point)

their


4. 

From _____ place I could watch the people eating _____ lunch.

(1 point)

my/their


5. 

I like _____ new car. I bought _____ yesterday.

(1 point)

my/it


6. 

He took off _____ jacket and loosened _____ tie.

(1 point)

his/his


7. 

My mum usually came to _____ office at 4 o'clock.

(1 point)

her


8. 

They've got two children but I don't remember _____ names.

(1 point)

their


9. 

We are going to invite all _____ friends to the party.

(1 point)

our


10. 

We're staying at a very nice hotel _____ room is very comfortable.

(1 point)

its
