DO_78_Подготовка к ОГЭ_So ans such, too and enough

Дополните каждое второе предложение так, чтобы по смыслу оно совпадало с первым


1. 

I have a lot of money and I don't know what to do with it. (such)

I have_________________that I don't know what to do with it. 

(1 point)

such a lot of money


2. 

Adrian fell asleep during the film because he was very tired. (so)

Adrian______________he fell asleep during the film. 

(1 point)

was so tired that


3. 

Today was so hot that I didn't want to do anything. (such)

Today was______________that I didn't want to do anything. 

(1 point)

such a hot day


4. 

I didn't have to open all my Christmas presents because I got so many! (such)

I got___________________Christmas presents that I didn't have time to open them all. 

(1 point)

such a lot of


5. 

They closed the funfair because it was very dangerous. (that) 

The funfair_______________they closed it.

(1 point)

was so dangerous that


6. 

The food was so spicy that I couldn't eat it. (such)

We had_______________that I couldn't eat it. 

(1 point)

such spicy food
