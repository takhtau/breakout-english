E_1.21. Present Simple Passive/Present Continuous Passive

Преобразуй слова в скобках так, чтобы они верно дополнили предложение


1. 

We (give) a lot of advice by our parents.

(1 point)

are given


2. 

All the contracts (sign) by the President.

(1 point)

are signed


3. 

You’ll have your copy soon, the contract (type).

(1 point)

is being typed


4. 

You can’t use the fax now, it (fix).

(1 point)

is being fixed


5. 

Lots of people (operate on) in this clinic. And now unfortunately my uncle John (operate) on here.

(1 point)

are operated on/is being operated on


6. 

Most cameras (make) in Japan.

(1 point)

are made


7. 

What are you doing here in the hall? – My room (clean).

(1 point)

is being cleaned


8. 

Can you hear footsteps behind? We (follow).

(1 point)

are being followed


9. 

This bill often (refer to).

(1 point)

is often referred to


10. 

He is such a nice chap. Why he so often (laugh at)?

(1 point)

is he so often laughed at
