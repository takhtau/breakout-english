С.7.1 сравнительная и превосходная степень прилагательных

Заполните пропуски, чтобы они грамматически соответствовали содержанию предложения.


1. 

She is a_____________tennis player. (GOOD) 

(1 point)

good


2. 

She plays tennis___________ . (GOOD) 

(1 point)

well


3. 

She is the___________tennis player in the group. (GOOD) 

(1 point)

best


4. 

Which is__________: Test One or Test Two? (EASY) 

(1 point)

easier


5. 

You will find the museum____________ . (EASY) 

(1 point)

easily


6. 

The flowers are_______________ . (BEAUTIFUL) 

(1 point)

beautiful


7. 

Australia is the_____________and_________continent. (SMALL/DRY) 

(1 point)

smallest/driest


8. 

Which is the_______________room in the hotel? (BEAUTIFUL)

(1 point)

most beautiful


9. 

Jerry's suitcase isn't very heavy, _____is_______________ . (YOU/HEAVY) 

(1 point)

yours/heavier


10. 

Seedless plants are the___________plants on the planet. (OLD) 

(1 point)

oldest


11. 

Test Five is not very easy. Test Four is much___________ . (EASY) 

(1 point)

easier
