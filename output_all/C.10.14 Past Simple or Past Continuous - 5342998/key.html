C.10.14 Past Simple or Past Continuous

Преобразуй слова в скобках в Past Simple or Past Continuous 


1. 

When I was young, I______________in the school football team. (PLAY)

(1 point)

played


2. 

My uncle Jim_________to Rome when I phoned him. (TRAVEL)

(1 point)

was travelling


3. 

Steve___________, had breakfast and then went to work. (GET DRESSED) 

(1 point)

got dressed


4. 

He____________when the lights went out. (COOK)

(1 point)

was cooking


5. 

David____________his bicycle while Kevin was skateboarding. (RIDE)

(1 point)

was riding


6. 

Dad___________the washing up before mum came home. (DO)

(1 point)

did


7. 

We_____________on holiday in June. (GO)

(1 point)

went


8. 

He____________the exam without difficulty. (PASS)

(1 point)

passed


9. 

A woman from Vancouver___________a bear in her kitchen. (FIND)

(1 point)

found


10. 

The detective asked, "What_____________at the time of the robbery?" (YOU/DO)

(1 point)

were you doing
