E_1.7. Future simple/continuous

Преобразуй слова в скобках так, чтобы они верно дополнили предложение


11. 

What about this camera? You (try) to take photos? It’s not allowed, you know... – No, I (try) to sell the camera.

(1 point)

 


12. 

He says he (meet) us at the bus stop, but I’m sure he (forget) about it.

(1 point)

 


13. 

He (come) if you ask him. When you (send) him the invitation?

(1 point)

 


14. 

You (do) anything special tomorrow? – Yes, my nephews (come) and I (show) them round London.

(1 point)

 


15. 

I hear you (go) to the regatta tomorrow. You (sail) in it?

(1 point)

 


16. 

My wedding (approach). I (tell) you the exact date soon.

(1 point)

 


17. 

Maria (work) today so I (get) the papers for her. 

(1 point)

 


18. 

Jane, you (come) or you (stay)?

(1 point)

 


19. 

I hear that you (get married) on Saturday. Your parents (come)?

(1 point)

 


20. 

The train (leave) in half an hour. We (take) a taxi not to be late for it.

(1 point)
