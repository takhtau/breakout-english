E_1.9. Past simple/continuous

Преобразуй слова в скобках так, чтобы они верно дополнили предложение


1. 

I (tell) him he should not read while he (eat).

(1 point)

told/was eating


2. 

The mouse (hide) while the cat (watch).

(1 point)

hid /was watching


3. 

The train (strike) an obstacle on the line, but it (not stop) because it (travel) too fast at the time.

(1 point)

struck/didn’t stop/was travelling


4. 

I (sit) in my garden yesterday, when the servant (come) and (say) that a visitor (wait) for me in the hall.

(1 point)

was sitting/came/said/was waiting


5. 

He (not like) to play while others (work).

(1 point)

didn’t like/were working


6. 

– Sorry, what you (say)? – I (not listen).

(1 point)

did you say/was not listening


7. 

You (enjoy) your game of tennis? – No, I (not play) as it (rain).

(1 point)

did you enjoy/didn’t play /was raining


8. 

Why you (tell) Ann her lessons (be) boring? – I (not mean) it. I only (joke).

(1 point)

did you tell/were/did not mean/was only joking


9. 

Why you (make) so many mistakes in the last test? – I (think) about my boyfriend.

(1 point)

did you make/was thinking
