DO_84_Подготовка к ОГЭ_Comparatives and superlatives

Перепишите предложения, используя соотв. форму слов, данных в скобках.


1. 

Joshua is much taller than Alex.

Alex is__________________ . 

(bad/beautiful/confident/fat/happy/near(to)/short/young)

(1 point)

shorter then Joshua


2. 

Theresa is more confident than Amy.

Amy is a lot______________ . 

(bad/beautiful/confident/fat/happy/near(to)/short/young)

(1 point)

less confident than Theresa


3. 

Your house is further from the school than mine.

My house is___________________ . 

(bad/beautiful/confident/fat/happy/near(to)/short/young)

(1 point)

nearer to the school than yours


4. 

Jude is less happy than Andy about the decision. 

Andy is___________________ . 

(bad/beautiful/confident/fat/happy/near(to)/short/young)

(1 point)

happier than Jude about decision


5. 

Bill is thinner than Simon.

Simon is_______________ . 

(bad/beautiful/confident/fat/happy/near(to)/short/young)

(1 point)

fatter than Bill


6. 

Terry is older than Sarah-Jane.

Sarah-Jane______________ . 

(bad/beautiful/confident/fat/happy/near(to)/short/young)

(1 point)

younger than Terry


7. 

Patricia is better than her sister on the clarinet. 

Patricia's sister is____________________ . 

(bad/beautiful/confident/fat/happy/near(to)/short/young)

(1 point)

worse than her


8. 

The houses here are uglier than in my grandparents' village.

The houses in my grandparents' village___________________ . 

(bad/beautiful/confident/fat/happy/near(to)/short/young)

(1 point)

are more beautiful than here
