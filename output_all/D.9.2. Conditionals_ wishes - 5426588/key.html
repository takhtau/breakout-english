D.9.2. Conditionals, wishes

Put the verbs in brackets in the correct tense. (-/-)


1. 

If you _____ (go) to bed earlier you _____ (not feel) so tired.

(1 point)

went/wouldn't feel


2. 

If I _____ (get) a promotion I _____ (not look for) another job.

(1 point)

had got/wouldn't look for


3. 

We _____ (have) plenty of time if it _____ (be) only 5 o'clock.

(1 point)

will have/is


4. 

If I _____ (be) very poor I _____ (be) upset.

(1 point)

were/wouldn't be


5. 

If it _____ (rain) I _____ (go) to see the races.

(1 point)

hadn't rained/would have gone


6. 

If I _____ (be invited) to the yesterday's party I _____ (go) there.

(1 point)

had been invited/would have gone


7. 

If I _____ (know) her well I _____ (phone) her at home.

(1 point)

knew/would phone


8. 

If the theatre _____ (not be) so far we _____ (walk) there.

(1 point)

were not/would walk


9. 

I _____ (buy) a Jaguar if I _____ (be) rich.

(1 point)

would buy/were


10. 

If you _____ (come) to my place I _____ (show) you my shots.

(1 point)

come/will show
