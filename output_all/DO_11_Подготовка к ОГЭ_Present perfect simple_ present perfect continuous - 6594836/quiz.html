DO_11_Подготовка к ОГЭ_Present perfect simple, present perfect continuous

Раскройте скобки, поставив глаголы в present perfect continuous. Используйте сокращенные формы глаголов там, где это возможно.


8. 

Mandy: Hi Matt. How are you? What______________(you/do) recently?

(1 point)

 


9. 

Matt: Oh, hi Mandy! Well,_______________(I/study) for my exams. 

(1 point)

 


10. 

Mandy: That sounds boring!______________(you/work) hard? 

(1 point)

 


11. 

Matt: Very! Basically,______________(I/just/sit) at my desk in y bedroom for the past three weeks and_____________(I/not/go) out at all. ___________(I/work) with Machel, my best friend, some of the time, though, so at least I've had some company. How about you? 

(1 point)

 


12. 

Mandy: Well, my mum and_____________(I/paint) my bedroom for the last few days. That has been fun! And______________(we/also/plan) our summer holiday. 

(1 point)

 


13. 

Matt: Great! Where are you going? 

Mandy: Well, we haven't decided yet. _______________(We/look) at different places to see which we like best. 

(1 point)

 


14. 

Matt: I'm sure you'll have a great time, whenever you go. Oh, by the way. ______________(I/think) of having a party when I finish my exams. Would you like to come?

Mandy: Sure! That would be great!

(1 point)
