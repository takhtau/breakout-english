DO_29_Подготовка к ОГЭ_The passive

Раскройте скобки, используя глаголы в соотв. временных формах страдательного залога. 


1. 

When people____________(arrest), they_____________(take) to the police station. 

(1 point)

are arrested/are taken


2. 

Milk____________(usually/keep) in the fridge. 

(1 point)

is usually kept


3. 

_____________(we/tell) what's in next week's test? 

(1 point)

Will we be told


4. 

How did people communicate over long distances before the phone____________(invent)? 

(1 point)

was invented


5. 

____________(you/allow) to come to the party next Saturday? 

(1 point)

Are you be allowed


6. 

You_____________(give) your exam results next Monday. 

(1 point)

will be given


7. 

______________((Aidan's bike/find) yesterday? 

(1 point)

Was Aidan's bike found
