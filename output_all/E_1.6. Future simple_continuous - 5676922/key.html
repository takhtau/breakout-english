E_1.6. Future simple/continuous

Преобразуй слова в скобках так, чтобы они верно дополнили предложение


1. 

Where you (go) for your next holiday?

(1 point)

are you going


2. 

I (give) you a lift to work tomorrow if you like.

(1 point)

will give


3. 

Do you know that Ann (come) at the end of the week?

(1 point)

is coming


4. 

 – I’m afraid I’m not quite ready. – Never mind. I (wait). 

(1 point)

will wait


5. 

Shop assistant: We have some very nice strawberries. Customer: All right. I (have) a pound.

(1 point)

will have


6. 

We (have) a party next Saturday night. You (come)?

(1 point)

are having/ will you come


7. 

Why you (take) the carpets down? You (paint) the walls? – No, I (take) the carpets to the cleaner’s.

(1 point)

are you taking/are you painting/am taking /will take


8. 

There are a lot of mistakes in this document. – All right. I (type) it again. 

(1 point)

will type


9. 

Leave a note for him on the table and he (see) it when he (come).

(1 point)

will see/comes


10. 

He (leave) for London tomorrow night. You (see) him off?

(1 point)

is leaving/will you see
