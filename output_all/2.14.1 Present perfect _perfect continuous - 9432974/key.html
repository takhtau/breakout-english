2.14.1 Present perfect /perfect continuous

Выбери один вариант ответа


1. 

He (to write) books since he was a young man.

(1 point)
      ⚫

has been writing 
      ⚪

have written 
      ⚪

wrote

2. 

What you (to do) here since morning?

(1 point)
      ⚪

have you done
      ⚫

have you been doing
      ⚪

did you do

3. 

Today she (to help) her mother since morning.

(1 point)
      ⚪

helped
      ⚪

has helped
      ⚫

has been helping 

4. 

They already (to wash) the floor and (to dust) the furniture.

(1 point)
      ⚫

have washed/dusted
      ⚪

have been washing/dusting
      ⚪

washed/dusted

5. 

He (to run) for ten minutes without any rest.

(1 point)
      ⚪

ran
      ⚫

has been running 
      ⚪

has run 

6. 

You (to find) your notebook?

(1 point)
      ⚫

Have you found
      ⚪

Have you been finding 
      ⚪

Did you find

7. 

I already (to look) for it for two hours, but (not to find) it yet.

(1 point)
      ⚪

have looked/haven't found
      ⚫

have been looking/haven't found
      ⚪

have been looking/haven't been finding 

8. 

I (to wait) for an answer from my cousin for a month already, but (not to receive) it yet.

(1 point)
      ⚫

have been waiting/haven't recieved 
      ⚪

have waited/haven't recieved
      ⚪

waited/recieved

9. 

She already (to do) her homework for two hours; but she (not to do) half of it yet.

(1 point)
      ⚪

did/ didn't do 
      ⚫

has been doing/hasn't done
      ⚪

has done/hasn't been doing

10. 

It is difficult for me to speak about this opera as I (not to hear) it.

(1 point)
      ⚪

have not been hearing
      ⚫

have not heard
      ⚪

didn't hear

11. 

I just (to receive) a letter from my granny, but I (not yet to receive) any letters from my parents.

(1 point)
      ⚫

have recieved/haven't recieved
      ⚪

recieved/didn't recieve 
      ⚪

have recieved/recieved

12. 

Jane already (to feed) the cat. You needn’t do it.

(1 point)
      ⚪

has been feeding 
      ⚫

have already fed
      ⚪

fed
