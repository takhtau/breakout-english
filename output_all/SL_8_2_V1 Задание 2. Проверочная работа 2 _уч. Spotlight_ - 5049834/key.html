SL/8_2_V1 Задание 2. Проверочная работа 2 (уч. Spotlight)

Заполните пропуски, чтобы они грамматически соответствовали содержанию предложения. При отрицании, используйте сокращенную форму (don't, haven't, won't).


1. 

They______________chess right now. They_____________chess since lunch. (PLAY/PLAY)

(1 point)

are playing/have been playing


2. 

When I_________the classroom yesterday they______________chess. (ENTER/PLAY) 

(1 point)

entered/were playing


3. 

He works at the zoo. He_________________there for three years already. (WORK) 

(1 point)

has been working


4. 

They are tired. They_______________since breakfast. (WORK)

(1 point)

have been working


5. 

I_________to Moscow next June. I_________always_________to go there. (GO/WANT)

(1 point)

will go/have/wanted


6. 

I____________to Moscow. I__________it three years ago. (BE/VISIT) 

(1 point)

have been/visited


7. 

Hurry up! Your classmates___________for you. They______________for ten minutes. (WAIT/WAIT) 

(1 point)

are waiting/have been waiting


8. 

I don't know where the museum is. I____________never_________there. (BE) 

(1 point)

have/been


9. 

The tsarina_________ill in 1560. (FALL)

(1 point)

fell


10. 

I_____________the news when suddenly the television___________itself off. (WATCH/SWITCH) 

(1 point)

was watching/switched


11. 

The tsar______________to change some old Russian customs. (TRY) 

(1 point)

tried


12. 

When the teacher__________the classroom all the children______________at their desks. Linda___________in the opposite direction and___________him. Suddenly she___________round. (ENTER/SIT/LOOK/SEE/TURN) 

(1 point)

entered/were sitting/looked/didn't see/turned


13. 

What________revenue________? (MEAN) 

(1 point)

does/mean


14. 

I___________the test! - Congratulations! (PASS) 

(1 point)

have passed


15. 

Oleg____________to the swimming pool. He prefers swimming in the river. (NOT GO)

(1 point)
