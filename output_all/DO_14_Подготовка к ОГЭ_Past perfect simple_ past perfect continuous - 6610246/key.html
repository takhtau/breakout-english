DO_14_Подготовка к ОГЭ_Past perfect simple, past perfect continuous

Поставь глаголы в past perfect simple


1. 

By the time I arrived, everyone____________(leave)!

(1 point)

had left


2. 

Steve_____________(already/see) the film, so he didn't come with us to the cinema. 

(1 point)

had already seen


3. 

Tina____________(not/finish) doing the housework by seven o'clock , so she called Andrea to tell her she would be late. 

(1 point)

hadn't finished


4. 

_______________(you/just/speak) to Billy when I rang? 

(1 point)

Had you just spoken


5. 

The car broke down just after_______________(we/set off). 

(1 point)

we'd set off


6. 

I didn't eat anything at the party because______________(I/already/eat) at home. 

(1 point)

I'd already eaten


7. 

_________________(you/hear) about the accident before you saw it on TV? 

(1 point)

Had you heard
