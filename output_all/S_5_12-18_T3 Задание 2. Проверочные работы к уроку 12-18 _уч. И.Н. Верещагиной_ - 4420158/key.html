S/5_12-18_T3 Задание 2. Проверочные работы к уроку 12-18 (уч. И.Н. Верещагиной)

Найди ошибки и напиши правильное предложение


1. 

Neither Betty nor Susan met nobody there.

(1 point)

Either Betty or Susan met nobody there.


2. 

Bill's sister said that she would have a test tomorrow.

(1 point)

Bill's sister said that she would have a test the next day.


3. 

Why is Mrs White look so angry? What has happened?

(1 point)

Why does Mrs White look so angry? What has happened?


4. 

I have never been to London. - So have I.

(1 point)

- Neither have I.


5. 

He said that he will visit his grandparents the next day.

(1 point)

He said that he would visit his grandparents the next day.


6. 

Everybody likes when it snows.

(1 point)

Everybody likes it when it snows.


7. 

These are the advice that Tom's teachers have given me.

(1 point)

This is the advice that Tom's teachers have given me.


8. 

I think we shouldn't invite them to our concert. 

(1 point)

I don't think we should invite them to our concert.


9. 

She tells that these newspapers are bought every day.

(1 point)

She tells that those newspapers are bought every day.


10. 

Mr Clark asks have you seen hos garden.

(1 point)

Mr Clark asks you have seen hos garden.
