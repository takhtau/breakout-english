E_1.4. Present simple/continuous

Преобразуй слова в скобках так, чтобы они верно дополнили предложение


1. 

He (become) an architect after he (graduate) from the University.

(1 point)

will become/graduates


2. 

I (have) to stop my daughter before she (get) into trouble.

(1 point)

will have/ gets


3. 

The new President (make) any changes in the country’s policy when he (come) to power?

(1 point)

will the President make/comes


4. 

Mum (visit) you when the baby-sitter (arrive).

(1 point)

will visit/arrives


5. 

She (relax) after she (hear) the results of the interview.

(1 point)

will relax/hears


6. 

My elder sister often (develop) a strong headache when the weather (change).

(1 point)

develops/changes


7. 

Tonight the old man (go) to bed as soon as the sun (set).

(1 point)

will go/sets


8. 

He (not buy) a new car until the company (pay) him all the money it (owe) him.

(1 point)

will not buy/pays/owes


9. 

The refugees (be able) to stay here till some urgent measures (take).

(1 point)

will be able/are taken


10. 

The information (study) carefully as soon as it (deliver).

(1 point)

will be studied/is delivered


11. 

Many people (be happy) when this ugly war (come) to an end.

(1 point)

will be happy/comes


12. 

Little Fred (not be able) to leave the house till his mother (come) back and (unlock) the door.

(1 point)

will not be able/comes/unlocks


13. 

Life (seem) wonderful to you again as soon as all your worries (be over).

(1 point)

will seem/are over


14. 

The weather certainly (change) for the better after the rain (stop) and the sky (clear up).

(1 point)

will certainly change/stops/clears up


15. 

He (not recover) soon unless he (take) the prescribed medicine.

(1 point)

will not recover/takes
