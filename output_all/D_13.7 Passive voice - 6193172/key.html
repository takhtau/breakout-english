D_13.7 Passive voice

Fill the gaps with the correct tenses (Active or Passive Voice).


1. 

In the year 122 AD, the Roman Emperor Hadrian (visit) _____ his provinces in Britain.

(1 point)

visited


2. 

On his visit, the Roman soldiers (tell) _____ him that Pictish tribes from Britain's north (attack) _____ them.

(1 point)

told/had attacked


3. 

So Hadrian (give) _____ the order to build a protective wall across one of the narrowest parts of the country.

(1 point)

gave


4. 

After 6 years of hard work, the Wall (finish) _____ in 128.

(1 point)

was finished


5. 

It (be) _____ 117 kilometres long and about 4 metres high.

(1 point)

was


6. 

The Wall (guard) _____ by 15,000 Roman soldiers.

(1 point)

was guarded


7. 

Every 8 kilometres there (be) _____ a large fort in which up to 1,000 soldiers (find) _____ shelter.

(1 point)

was/found


8. 

The soldiers (watch) _____ over the frontier to the north and (check) _____ the people who (want) _____ to enter or leave Roman Britain.

(1 point)

watched/wanted


9. 

In order to pass through the Wall, people (have to go) _____ to one of the small forts that (serve) _____ as gateways.

(1 point)

had to go/served


10. 

Those forts (call) _____ milecastles because the distance from one fort to another (be) _____ one Roman mile (about 1,500 metres).

(1 point)

were called/was
