C.1.12 Mix TENSES

Заполните пропуски, чтобы они грамматически соответствовали содержанию предложения. При отрицании используй сокращенную форму (don't, won,t, haven't).


1. 

The police________still____________for the stolen car. They_______________it yet. (LOOK/NOT FIND)

(1 point)

are/looking/haven't found


2. 

I think Grandpa___________with me after he___________his books. (PLAY/UNPACK)

(1 point)

will play/unpack


3. 

He___________the dictionary while he_____________the books. (FIND/PACK) 

(1 point)

found/was packing


4. 

I want him____________the clothes. (PACK) 

(1 point)

to pack


5. 

She made him____________the clothes. (PACK)

(1 point)

pack


6. 

He______________the clothes before he called a taxi. (PACK)

(1 point)

had packed


7. 

Who__________clothes? - I do. (PACK) 

(1 point)

packs


8. 

What_____you___________now? It smells good. (COOK)

(1 point)

are/cooking


9. 

When________you_____________porridge last time? (COOK)

(1 point)

did/cook


10. 

He works in the library. He______________there for seven years. (WORK)

(1 point)

has been working


11. 

He___________the printer later this month. (BUY) 

(1 point)

will buy


12. 

The printer_____________later this month. (BUY) 

(1 point)

will be bought


13. 

Next month she__________fifteen years old. (BE) 

(1 point)

will be


14. 

We____________the meeting until everybody___________ . (NOT BEGIN/COME) 

(1 point)

won't begin/comes


15. 

If he____________a taxi, he_________on time. (TAKE/BE)

(1 point)

takes/will be


16. 

In 1666 the fire_____________three-quarters of the city. (DESTROY) 

(1 point)

destroyed


17. 

The Cathedral_____________in 1931. (DESTROY) 

(1 point)

was destroyed


18. 

__________crocodiles___________to the same group as alligators? (BELONG) 

(1 point)

Do/belong
