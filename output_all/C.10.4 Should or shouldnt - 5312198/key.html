C.10.4 Should or shouldn't

Используй подсказки, чтобы написать верные советы, используя should or shouldn't 


1. 

I lost my red pen. (keep/pencil case)

(1 point)

You should keep it in your pencil case.


2. 

I feel really sick. (play/outside) 

(1 point)

You shouldn't play outside.


3. 

I've got a toothache. (see/dentist) 

(1 point)

You should see a dentist.


4. 

I'm very worried about my exams! (worry so much) 

(1 point)

You shouldn't worry so much.


5. 

I broke my brother's new camera. (tell/tonight) 

(1 point)

You should tell him tonight.


6. 

I'm very worried about my best friend. (worry so much) 

(1 point)

You shouldn't worry so much.


7. 

I've got a bad headache. (take/painkiller)

(1 point)

You should take a painkiller.


8. 

I've broken my sister's new mobile phone. (tell/tonight) 

(1 point)

You should tell her tonight.


9. 

It's very cold today. (wear/a jackrt)

(1 point)

You should wear a jacket.


10. 

I feel very tired. (stay up/late)

(1 point)

You shouldn't stay up late.
