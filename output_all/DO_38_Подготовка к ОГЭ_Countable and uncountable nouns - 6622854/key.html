DO_38_Подготовка к ОГЭ_Countable and uncountable nouns

Дополните второе предложение так, чтобы оно совпадало по смыслу с первым.


1. 

We don't know anything about the problem. (information) 

We don't______________about the problem. 

(1 point)

have any information


2. 

Is it okay if I have some cheese? (bit) 

Is it okay if I have_______________cheese? 

(1 point)

a bit of


3. 

There's only a little coffee left in the jar. (much) 

There____________coffee left in the jar. 

(1 point)

isn't much


4. 

I try not to drink too much Coca-Cola in a week. (cans) 

I try not to drink too____________of Coca-Cola in a week. 

(1 point)

many cans


5. 

Would you like some more chocolate? (piece)

Would you like____________chocolate? 

(1 point)

another piece of


6. 

I don't want a lot of cream on my strawberries. (cream) 

I only want____________on my strawberries. 

(1 point)

a little cream
