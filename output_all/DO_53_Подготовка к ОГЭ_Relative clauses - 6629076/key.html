DO_53_Подготовка к ОГЭ_Relative clauses

Из 2-х предложений составьте одно, используя неограничительное придаточное предложение. 


1. 

Friendly People is a comedy. It's my favourite programme. 

(1 point)

Friendly People which is my favourite programme is a comedy


2. 

My friend Michael often comes to play with me. He hasn't got any brothers and sisters. 

(1 point)

My friend Michael, who hasn't got any brothers and sisters, often comes to play with me


3. 

My sister loves wearing hats. Her hair is brown. 

(1 point)

My sister, whose hair is brown, loves wearing hats


4. 

New York is an enormous city. It's where I was born. 

(1 point)

New York, where I was born is an enormous city


5. 

This CD is scratched. I only bought it yesterday. 

(1 point)

This CD which I only bought yesterday is scratched


6. 

My brother George has got some great shirts! He hates me borrowing his clothes. 

(1 point)

My brother George, who hates me borrowing his clothes, has got some great shirts!


7. 

Our neighbours have never invited us to dinner. Their house is directly opposite ours. 

(1 point)

Our neighbours, whose house is directly opposite ours, have never invited us to dinner
