D_13.6 Passive voice

Complete the sentences (Active or Passive Voice)


1. 

The Statue of Liberty (give) _____ to the United States by France.

(1 point)

was given


2. 

It (be) _____ a present on the 100th anniversary of the United States.

(1 point)

was


3. 

The Statue of Liberty (design) _____ by Frederic Auguste Bartholdi.

(1 point)

was designed


4. 

It (complete) _____ in France in July 1884.

(1 point)

was completed


5. 

In 350 pieces, the statue then (ship) _____ to New York, where it (arrive) _____ on June 17th, 1885.

(1 point)

was shipped/arrived


6. 

The pieces (put) _____ together and the opening ceremony (take) _____ place on October 28th, 1886.

(1 point)

were put/took


7. 

The Statue of Liberty (be) _____ 46 m high (93 m including the base).

(1 point)

is


8. 

The statue (represent) _____ the goddess of liberty.

(1 point)

represents


9. 

She (hold) _____ a torch in her right hand and a tablet in her left hand.

(1 point)

holds


10. 

On the tablet you (see/can) _____ the date of the Declaration of Independence (July 4, 1776). Every year, the Statue of Liberty (visit) _____ by many people from all over the world.

(1 point)

can see/is visited
