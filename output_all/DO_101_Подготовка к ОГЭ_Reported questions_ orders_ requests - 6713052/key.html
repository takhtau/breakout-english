DO_101_Подготовка к ОГЭ_Reported questions, orders, requests

Дополните каждое второе предложение так, чтобы по смыслу оно совпадало с первым


1. 

'When did your brother join the army?' Rudy asked me. 

Rudy asked me_______________the army. 

(1 point)

when my brother had joined


2. 

'How was your trip to Turkey?' I asked Katherine. 

I asked Katherine how______________been. 

(1 point)

her trip to Turkey had


3. 

'How will we find your keys in the dark?' Ashley asked Susie. 

Ashley asked Susie_____________keys in the dark. 

(1 point)

how they would find her


4. 

'Why was Rob on TV?' Denise asked me. 

Danice asked me_____________on TV. 

(1 point)

why Rob had been


5. 

"Who saw you at park?' the police officer asked me. 

The police officer asked me____________at the park. 

(1 point)

who had seen me


6. 

"Who did you see at the park?' the police officer asked me. 

The police officer asked me____________at the park. 

(1 point)

who I had seen
