D.1.21. Passive voice

Change the following sentences into the Passive.


1. 

They found the rare manuscript in the basement.

(1 point)

The rare manuscript was found in the basement.


2. 

Do they include the service charge?

(1 point)

Is the service charge included?


3. 

I bought a book a week ago.

(1 point)

The book was bought a week ago.


4. 

The scientists are examining the new bacterium.

(1 point)

A new bacterium is being examined by the scientists.


5. 

An old lady found a box of jewellery last week.

(1 point)

The box of jewellery was found by the old lady last week.


6. 

People often ask for this book.

(1 point)

The book is often asked for.


7. 

A distinguished surgeon will operate on my sister.

(1 point)

My sister will be operated on by a distinguished surgeon.


8. 

Tom has just told me a good story.

(1 point)

A good story has just been told me by Tom.


9. 

She has tidied the room thoroughly.

(1 point)

The room has been tidied thoroughly.


10. 

Bob and Jack were carrying our bags.

(1 point)

Our bags were being carried by Bob and Jack.
