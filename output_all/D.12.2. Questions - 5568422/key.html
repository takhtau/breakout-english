D.12.2. Questions

Make questions from the following words.


1. 

are/film/see/going/What/you/to

(1 point)

What film are you going to see?


2. 

go/me/alone/Why/out/you/and/don't/leave

(1 point)

Why don't you go and leave me alone?


3. 

main/like/does/What/look/actress/the

(1 point)

What does the main actress look like?


4. 

I/to/if/Why/music/to/listen/I/want/shouldn't

(1 point)

Why shouldn't I listen to music if I want to?


5. 

of/daily/television/routine/part/Is/your

(1 point)

Is television part of your daily routine?


6. 

think/right/to/all/Do/it/of/throw/things/you/was/Jim/away/his

(1 point)

Do you think it was right of Jim to throw away all his things?


7. 

name/the/ever/play/is/of/you/best/What/have/the/seen

(1 point)

What is the name of the best play you have ever seen?


8. 

washing/do/How/times/week/you/many/the/up/a/do

(1 point)

How many times a week do you do the washing up?


9. 

where/you/the/is/nearest/Do/post office/know

(1 point)

Do you know where the nearest post office is?


10. 

you/speaking/is/Can/guess/who

(1 point)

Can you guess who is speaking?
