DE_30_Reported speech

Rewrite as reported questions, beginning with the words given. 


1. 

"Have you had your ear pierced?"

My mum asked me if___________________________ .

(1 point)

I had had my ear pierced


2. 

"Can I meet you there at six o'clock tonight, Doug?"

Julian wanted to know whether____________________________ .

(1 point)

he could meet Doug there at six o'clock that night


3. 

"Are you still moving to Blackpool next week?"

Fiona asked Rod and Jenny whether______________________ .

(1 point)

they were still moving to Blackpool the following week


4. 

"Does Graham have to wear a suit to work?"

I asked Mrs Daley if Graham________________________________ .

(1 point)

has to wear a suit to work


5. 

"Do you want someone to feed your cat while you're away?"

Jan asked me if ________________________ .

(1 point)

I wanted someone to feed my cat while I was away


6. 

"Can you guess what I've given Lindsay for her birthday?"

Daisy asked Wendy if________________________________ .

(1 point)

she could guess what she'd given Lindsay for her birthday


7. 

"Do you love me or not, Gloria?"

Fred asked Gloria whether________________________ .

(1 point)

she loves him or not


8. 

"Will Simon be coming to the party tomorrow night?"

Adrian wondered whether________________________ .

(1 point)

Simon would be coming to the party the following night


9. 

"Tell me if you were anywhere near 34 Aylesford Street last night!"

The police officer demanded to know whether Sykes___________________________________ .

(1 point)

had been anywhere near 34 Aylesford Street the previous night
