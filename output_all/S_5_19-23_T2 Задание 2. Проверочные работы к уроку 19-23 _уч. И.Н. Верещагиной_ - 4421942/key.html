S/5_19-23_T2 Задание 2. Проверочные работы к уроку 19-23 (уч. И.Н. Верещагиной)

Найди ошибки и напиши правильное предложение


1. 

The dwarfs told Snow White that she could live in our house.

(1 point)

The dwarfs told Snow White that she could live in their house.


2. 

He said he didn't hear anything about that place before.

(1 point)

He said he hadn't heard anything about that place before.


3. 

Cinderella's stepsister said their mother that they wanted to have new clothes.

(1 point)

Cinderella's stepsister said to their mother that they wanted to have new clothes.


4. 

There was an armchair in the room.

There was very comfortable.

(1 point)

It was very comfortable.


5. 

The prince asked the girl don't be afraid.

(1 point)

The prince asked the girl not to be afraid.


6. 

The girl asked the bear where will he go.

(1 point)

The girl asked the bear where would he go.


7. 

Snow White said that she will never forget him.

(1 point)

Snow White said that she would never forget him.


8. 

Cinderella said that she could not wear these old clothes.

(1 point)

Cinderella said that she could not wear those old clothes.


9. 

The horse is one of the most beautiful animal in the world., isn't it?

(1 point)

The horse is one of the most beautiful animals in the world., isn't it?


10. 

Fairy asked the girl was the dress beautiful.

(1 point)

Fairy asked the girl if the dress was beautiful.
