S/4_37-49_V1 Задание 2. Проверочные работы к уроку 37-49-8 (уч. И.Н. Верещагиной)

Найдите ошибки и напишите правильное предложение


1. 

I have learnt the words on the page ten but I haven't read the text.

(1 point)

I have learnt the words on page ten but I haven't read the text.


2. 

When has your mother left school?

(1 point)

When did your mother leave school?


3. 

Bill is ill. He is ill for three days.

(1 point)

Bill is ill. He has been ill for three days.


4. 

Five millions people live in this city.

(1 point)

Five million people live in this city.


5. 

Could you buy little jam, please?

(1 point)

Could you buy a little jam, please?


6. 

I want to say you something funny.

(1 point)

I want to say to you something funny.


7. 

What time is the lesson begin?

(1 point)

What time does the lesson begin?


8. 

How many sandwiches have you made yesterday?

(1 point)

How many sandwiches did you make yesterday?


9. 

We have lived here for 2020.

(1 point)

We have lived here since 2020.


10. 

You must eat less hamburgers.

(1 point)

You should eat less hamburgers.
