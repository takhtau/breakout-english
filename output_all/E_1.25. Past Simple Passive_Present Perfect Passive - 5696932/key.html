E_1.25. Past Simple Passive/Present Perfect Passive

Преобразуй слова в скобках так, чтобы они верно дополнили предложение


1. 

You ever (teach) to play chess?

(1 point)

have you ever been taught


2. 

I just (advise) to keep to a diet.

(1 point)

have just been advised


3. 

The sportsmen (give) instructions before the match.

(1 point)

were given


4. 

The motorist (disqualify) some five months ago.

(1 point)

was disqualified


5. 

I can’t believe my eyes! My book (publish) already!

(1 point)

has been published already


6. 

I am not going to the party. I (not invite).

(1 point)

have not been invited


7. 

The first baths (build) by the Romans.

(1 point)

were built


8. 

Where is my bicycle? It’s gone. It (steal).

(1 point)

has been stolen


9. 

This room looks different. It (paint) since I was last here?

(1 point)

has it been painted


10. 

Why have you come today? – The date of the meeting (change).

(1 point)

has been changed
