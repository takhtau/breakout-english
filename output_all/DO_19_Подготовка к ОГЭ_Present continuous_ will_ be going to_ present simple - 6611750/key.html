DO_19_Подготовка к ОГЭ_Present continuous, will, be going to, present simple

Раскройте скобки, используя соответствующие формы be going to.  Некоторые предложения могут быть отрицательными. 


1. 

When I grow up, I______________(play) guitar in a rock group! 

(1 point)

am going to play


2. 

Rick and Mark______________(start) going to the gym twice a week. 

(1 point)

are going to start


3. 

______________Lauren__________(tell) her mum about what happened? 

(1 point)

Is/going to tell


4. 

I______________(look) on the internet for information about snowboarding. 

(1 point)

am going to look


5. 

No, Nadie____________(invite) everyone from class - just her close friends. 

(1 point)

isn't going to invite


6. 

_____________Harry_____________(be) ready on time or not? 

(1 point)

Is/going to be


7. 

Careful! You_____________(break) something with that ball! Go outside!

(1 point)

are going to break


8. 

I_______________(lie down) for half an hour. Call me at six o'clock. 

(1 point)

am going to lie down
