D.11.1. Nouns, Adjectives, Adverbs

Rewrite the sentences using one of the words in brackets.


1. 

Do you want to go there? (real/really)

(1 point)

Do you really want to go there?


2. 

I can rewrite this article. (easy/easily)

(1 point)

I can rewrite this article easily.


3. 

Alice danced too. (pretty/prettily)

(1 point)

Alice danced too prettily.


4. 

That was an answer. (angry/angrily)

(1 point)

That was an angry answer.


5. 

We have heard very little of him. (late/lately)

(1 point)

We have heard very little of him lately.


6. 

They sell these books. (dear/dearly)

(1 point)

They sell these books dear.


7. 

They had a talk yesterday. (pleasant/pleasantly)

(1 point)

They had a pleasant talk yesterday.


8. 

Alec drives the car. (careful/carefully)

(1 point)

Alec drives the car carefully.


9. 

His English is. (fluent/fluently)

(1 point)

His English is fluent.


10. 

I am satisfied. (complete/completely)

(1 point)

I am completely satisfied.
