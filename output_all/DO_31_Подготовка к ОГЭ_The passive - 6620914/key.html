DO_31_Подготовка к ОГЭ_The passive

Дополните второе предложение так, чтобы оно совпадало по смыслу с первым 


1. 

Will they send the letters first class? (sent)

Will____________first class? 

(1 point)

the letters be sent


2. 

I'm not sure if they eat pizza in China. (is)

I'm not sure if______________in China. 

(1 point)

pizza is eaten


3. 

Someone told me that they don't make cars in the UK anymore. (made)

Someone told me that______________in the UK anymore. 

(1 point)

cars aren't made


4. 

Do they usually feed the animals three times a day? (fed)

Are_____________three times a day? 

(1 point)

the animals usually fed


5. 

Mr Jones is ill, so he won't give us a geography test today! (be)

Mr Jones is ill, so____________a geography test today!

(1 point)

we won't be given


6. 

Did they take her to hospital in an ambulance? (she)

Was_____________to hospital in an ambulance? 

(1 point)

she taken
