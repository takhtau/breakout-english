2.13.3 Present simple/continuous/perfect

Выберите правильный вариант ответа


1. 

The children (to run) at the stadium every day.

(1 point)
      ⚫

run
      ⚪

are running
      ⚪

have run

2. 

They (to sing) merrily at the moment.

(1 point)
      ⚪

sing
      ⚫

are singing
      ⚪

have sung

3. 

We ... just ... (to learn) a new song!

(1 point)
      ⚪

do/learn
      ⚪

are/learning
      ⚫

have/learned

4. 

... you ... (to work) right now?

(1 point)
      ⚫

Are/working
      ⚪

Do/work
      ⚪

Have/worked

5.

(1 point)
      ⚪

am preparing
      ⚫

prepare
      ⚪

have prepared

6.

(1 point)
      ⚪

am/taking
      ⚪

do/take
      ⚫

have/taken

7.

(1 point)
      ⚫

has/ordered
      ⚪

      ⚪

8.

      ⚪

      ⚫

      ⚪

      ⚪

... you already ... (to write) your essay?

      ⚫

      ⚪

      ⚪
