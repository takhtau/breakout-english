S/4_37-49_V2 Задание 2. Проверочные работы к уроку 37-49 (уч. И.Н. Верещагиной)

Найди ошибки и напиши правильное предложение


1. 

Two years ago he bought a lot of books. Now he buys less books.

(1 point)

Now he buys fewer books.


2. 

I have translated the dialogue, but I haven't learnt the words already.

(1 point)

I have translated the dialogue, but I haven't learnt the words yet.


3. 

They have lived in London since two years.

(1 point)

They have lived in London for two years.


4. 

The film is going to begin at 9:30.

(1 point)

The film will begin at 9:30.


5. 

When have you written the dictation?

(1 point)

When did you write the dictation?


6. 

What have you done yesterday?

(1 point)

What did you do yesterday?


7. 

Million of people collect stamps.

(1 point)

Millions of people collect stamps.


8. 

What did Mr Smith's son tell?

(1 point)

What did Mr Smith's son say?


9. 

It is no letter for Mr Brown today.

(1 point)

There is no letter for Mr Brown today.


10. 

I saw him few minutes ago.

(1 point)

I saw him a few minutes ago.
