С.8.4 Местоимения

Заполните пропуски, чтобы они грамматически соответствовали содержанию предложения.


1. 

- Who told________the news? (SHE/SHE)

- A friend of________ .

(1 point)

her/hers


2. 

Sally's textbook is on the desk. Where is________? (I) 

(1 point)

mine


3. 

I'll speak to Sally___________ . (I) 

(1 point)

myself


4. 

Sally told_________everything. (I) 

(1 point)

me


5. 

Boys, you'll enjoy__________if you travel by train. (YOU) 

(1 point)

yourselves


6. 

Who will give_________the dictionary? (WE) 

(1 point)

us


7. 

Come and meet Julia. She's a good friend of__________ . (I) 

(1 point)

mine


8. 

Some fish give off electric shocks to protect______________ . (THEY) 

(1 point)

themselves


9. 

Jane is an old friend of________ . (I) 

(1 point)

mine


10. 

Did Jane enjoy__________at the concert last night? (SHE) 

(1 point)

herself


11. 

Jane, did you enjoy________________at the concert last night? (YOU)

(1 point)

yourself


12. 

________room is larger than_________ . (HE/I)

(1 point)

His/mine


13. 

"Swan Lake" was not a success at_________first performance. (IT) 

(1 point)

its


14. 

Jane, try it______________ . (YOU) 

(1 point)

yourself


15. 

Jane and Tom, try it_________________ . (YOU) 

(1 point)

yourselves
