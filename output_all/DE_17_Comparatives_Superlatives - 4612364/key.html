DE_17_Comparatives/Superlatives

Поставь слова в скобках в правильную форму


1. 

I think Mohammed Ali was the__________(great) boxer that ever lived.

(1 point)

greatest


2. 

It was the_________(boring) film I had ever seen.

(1 point)

most boring


3. 

Our teacher told us that the student with the___________(high) mark would get a prize.

(1 point)

highest


4. 

Ginger is on of the________(lucky) cats alive - he's been hit by the car three times and has never been hurt!

(1 point)

luckiest


5. 

Out of all the students in my music school, I practise_____________(often).

(1 point)

the most often


6. 

The Pacific is the___________(deep) ocean in the world.

(1 point)

deepest


7. 

That's the__________(ugly) fish I've ever seen!

(1 point)

ugliest


8. 

I was ill before the exam and I did__________(badly) out of the whole class.

(1 point)

the worst


9. 

The__________(far) I've run in one day is about ten kilometres. 

(1 point)

farthest


10. 

Don't you think this would be the_________(lovely) spot for picnic?

(1 point)

loveliest


11. 

But that's the_________(crazy) idea I've ever heard!

(1 point)

craziest


12. 

I asked the assistant to show me the_________(modern) phone they had.

(1 point)

most modern


13. 

I told the manager that it was the__________(bad) hotel I had ever stayed in.

(1 point)

worst


14. 

Who do you think is the___________(little) talented actor to win an Oscar?

(1 point)

least


15. 

Out of all the members of the choir, Jason sang_____________(well).

(1 point)

the best
