DO_109_Подготовка к ОГЭ_Wish

Раскройте скобки, поставив глаголы в соответствующую форму


1. 

I wish I___________(have) a million euros! I'd buy lots of great things.

(1 point)

had


2. 

I wish we__________(live) in a bigger house. This one is too small. 

(1 point)

lived


3. 

Becca wishes she_____________(be) old enough to drive a car. 

(1 point)

was


4. 

Do you wish you__________(feel) more confident about the exam tomorrow. 

(1 point)

felt


5. 

I wish my computer____________(not/be) broken. I can't check my e-mail. 

(1 point)

wasn't


6. 

I wish I_____________(not/like) chocolate so much! I eat three bars a day! 

(1 point)

didn't like


7. 

Grant wishes he_______________(not/make) so many mistakes all the time. 

(1 point)

didn't make


8. 

Look what they're wearing! I bet they wish they____________(not/look) so silly!

(1 point)

didn't look
