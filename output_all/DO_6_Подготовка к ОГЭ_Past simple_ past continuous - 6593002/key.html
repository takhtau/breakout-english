DO_6_Подготовка к ОГЭ_Past simple, past continuous

Раскройте скобки, поставив глаголы в past simple. 


1. 

Ted____________(play) his guitar at half past seven. 

(1 point)

was playing


2. 

At midnight, I_______________(sleep), but Jane______________(listen) to music. 

(1 point)

was sleeping/was listening


3. 

Luke____________(stand) outside the bank when suddenly two robbers ran past him. 

(1 point)

was standing


4. 

I know Doug_____________(work) late at the office because I saw him when I_____________(leave). 

(1 point)

was working/was leaving


5. 

____________you_____________(have) a shower when the earthquake happened? 

(1 point)

Were/having


6. 

Penny______________(run) to catch the bus when she slipped and fell. 

(1 point)

was running


7. 

When you saw Eugene___________he____________(go) home? 

(1 point)

was/going


8. 

At midnight? Em........we_______________(watch) a DVD, I think. 

(1 point)

were watching
