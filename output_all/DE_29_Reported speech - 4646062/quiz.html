DE_29_Reported speech

Complete each second sentence using the word given, so that it has a similar meaning to the first sentence. Write between two and five words in each gap.


11. 

"We'll be travelling round Europe next month" said Jerry.

Jerry said that___________________be travelling round Europe. (FOLLOWING)

(1 point)

 


12.

(1 point)

 


13.
