DO_15_Подготовка к ОГЭ_Past perfect simple, past perfect continuous

Из 2-х вариантов выберите предложения, синонимичные предложениям 1-7.


8. 

We'd had dinner when Wendy arrived. 

(1 point)
      ⚪

Wendy arrived and then we had dinner. 
      ⚪

We had dinner and then Wendy arrived. 

9. 

I read the book after I've seen the film. 

(1 point)
      ⚪

I saw the film and then I read the book. 
      ⚪

I read the book and then I saw the film. 

10. 

By the time Dad came home, I'd gone to bed. 

(1 point)
      ⚪

I went to bed before Dad came home. 
      ⚪

I went to bed after Dad came home. 

11. 

She didn't go to bed until her mum had come home. 

(1 point)
      ⚪

She went to bed and then her mum came home. 
      ⚪

Her mum came home and then she went to bed. 

12. 

Mr Banks hadn't arrived at the office by the time I got there. 

(1 point)
      ⚪

I arrived before Mr Banks. 
      ⚪

Mr Banks arrived before me. 

13. 

They'd bought the plane tickets before they heard about the cheaper flight. 

(1 point)
      ⚪

They bought the plane tickets and later they heard about the cheaper flight. 
      ⚪

They heard about the cheaper flight and then they bought the plane tickets. 

14. 

The girls had tidied the house when the visitors arrived. 

(1 point)
      ⚪

The visitors arrived and later the girls tidied the house. 
      ⚪

The girls tidied the house and then the visitors arrived.
