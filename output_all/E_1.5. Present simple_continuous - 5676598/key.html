E_1.5. Present simple/continuous

Преобразуй слова в скобках так, чтобы они верно дополнили предложение


1. 

Today he (tell) you if he (play) chess with you on Saturday evening.

(1 point)

will tell/will play


2. 

We (be) not sure if this friendship (last) long. 

(1 point)

are not/will last


3. 

I wonder when he (return) my finest collection of records.

(1 point)

will return


4. 

Many people believe that the time (come) when there (be) a universal parliament of the world.

(1 point)

will come/will be


5. 

The poor fellow (wonder) if the next mail (bring) better news from home.

(1 point)

wonders/will bring


6. 

I (not know) if the local people (be) friendly to my family in future.

(1 point)

don’t know/will be


7. 

The secretary (want) to know if the new Head (make) any changes.

(1 point)

wants/will make


8. 

The doctor can’t say now when the patient (recover).

(1 point)

will recover


9. 

Tell me if you (finish) your book in summer and when exactly you (do) it.

(1 point)

will finish/will do


10. 

People standing on the bus stop (wonder) if it (stop) raining soon.

(1 point)

wonder/will stop


11. 

Nobody (know) if he (keep) his promise.

(1 point)

knows/will keep


12. 

I wonder if he (be) late again.

(1 point)

will be


13. 

My dressmaker (have) no idea when my wedding dress (be) ready.

(1 point)

has/will be


14. 

Why are you measuring the cooker? – I’d like to see if it (fit) in my new kitchen.

(1 point)

will fit


15. 

Tell me the exact time when they (come).

(1 point)

will come
