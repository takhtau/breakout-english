E_1.10. Past simple/Present Perfect

Преобразуй слова в скобках так, чтобы они верно дополнили предложение


1. 

He (forget) his French since he (leave) Paris.

(1 point)

has forgotten/left


2. 

Years ago he (be) very poor, and (not know) how to live. He (become) very rich now.

(1 point)

was/didn’t know/has become


3. 

I can reach my work easily now, as I (buy) a new car.

(1 point)

have bought


4. 

– The man you see there is the man to whom I (lend) the money. – When you (lend) it to him? – I (do) it yesterday.

(1 point)

have lent/did you lend/did


5. 

I (lose) my keys and cannot remember where I (see) them last.

(1 point)

have lost/saw


6. 

He cannot see well as he (become) short-sighted.

(1 point)

has become


7. 

He is a man who (live) a remarkable life.

(1 point)

has lived


8. 

What you (do) last night?

(1 point)

did you do


9. 

What you (do) since I last (see) you?

(1 point)

have you done/saw


10. 

I (not play) much football since I (leave) school.

(1 point)

haven’t played/left
