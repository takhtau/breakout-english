DO_66_Подготовка к ОГЭ_Modals 3: the modal perfect

Дополните каждое второе предложение так, чтобы по смыслу оно совпадало с первым


1. 

I expect Adrian did a lot of revision. (must)

Adrian_________________a lot of revision. 

(1 point)

must have done


2. 

I don't believe that Jim stole the money. (can't) 

Jim______________the money. 

(1 point)

can't have stolen


3. 

It's possible that I have made a mistake. (could)

I______________a mistake. 

(1 point)

could have made


4. 

There's a chance that someone saw us. (may) 

Someone_______________us. 

(1 point)

may have seen


5. 

It's possible that Tim hasn't arrived yet. (might)

Tim_______________yet. 

(1 point)

might not have arrived


6. 

I'm sure that Irene wasn't at the party because she was ill. (have) 

Irene______________at the party because she was ill.

(1 point)

can't have been
