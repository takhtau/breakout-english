S/5_1-5_T1 Задание 2. Проверочные работы к уроку 1-5 (уч. И.Н. Верещагиной)

Найди ошибки и напиши правильное предложение


1. 

I visit my grandparents two times the week.

(1 point)

I visit my grandparents two times a week.


2. 

I'll at home at eight o'clock tomorrow.

(1 point)

I'll be at home at eight o'clock tomorrow.


3. 

- How long do they know the doctor?

- For five years.

(1 point)

How long have they known the doctor?


4. 

There is not cheese in this fridge.

(1 point)

There is not any cheese in this fridge.


5. 

- Who have soup for dinner?

- We have.

(1 point)

- Who has soup for dinner?


6. 

I am sure we are going to have a dictation tomorrow.

(1 point)

I am sure we shall have a dictation tomorrow.


7. 

- What did she gave you?

- A book.

(1 point)

- What did she give you?


8. 

These children have bought Book Two last Saturday.

(1 point)

These children bought Book Two last Saturday.


9. 

He is siting on the teacher's chair now.

(1 point)

He is sitting on the teacher's chair now.


10. 

How long are they going to stay in these city.

(1 point)

How long are they going to stay in this city.
