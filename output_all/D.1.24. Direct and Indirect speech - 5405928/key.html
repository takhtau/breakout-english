D.1.24. Direct and Indirect speech

Report the statements.


1. 

Betty said to Lucy, "I'm sorry to disturb you."

(1 point)

Betty told Lucy she was sorry to disturb her.


2. 

Dan said, "I've missed my bus, I'll be late and my boss will be furious."

(1 point)

Dan said he had missed his bus, he would be late and his boss would be furious.


3. 

The girl said to her friends, "I didn't go to the stadium yesterday."

(1 point)

The girl told her friends she hadn't gone to the stadium the previous day.


4. 

My brother said, "I'm talking on the phone."

(1 point)

My brother said he was talking on the phone.


5. 

Nora says to Jack, "I'll tell you what to do."

(1 point)

Nora tells Jack she will tell him what to do.


6. 

Lora said, "I wish I had something to eat."

(1 point)

Lora said she wished she had something to eat.


7. 

Bill said, "I've been ill for a fortnight."

(1 point)

Bill said he had been ill for a fortnight.


8. 

She said, "I've never set eyes on him in my life."

(1 point)

She said she had never set eyes on him in her life.


9. 

He said, "Bill wants to know if you are free tomorrow."

(1 point)

He said Bill wanted to know if she were free the next day.


10. 

Jane says, "When the doorbell rang I was playing the violin."

(1 point)

Jane says when the doorbell rang she was playing a violin.
