D.1.25. Direct and Indirect speech

Report the questions.


1. 

She asked him, "Why did you make me go out tonight?"

(1 point)

She asked him why he had made her go out that night.


2. 

I said to Jim, "Where were you last month?"

(1 point)

I asked Jim where he had been the previous month.


3. 

She says to me, "Do you think it is right?"

(1 point)

She asks me if I think it is right.


4. 

The writer said to the editor, "Will the book have been published by spring?"

(1 point)

The writer asked the editor if the book would have been published by spring.


5. 

He says, "What are you doing there, Liz?"

(1 point)

He asks Liz what she is doing there.


6. 

She asked me, "Are these articles still being printed?"

(1 point)

She asked me if those articles were still being printed.


7. 

He said to her, "Why don't you spend a few days with Alice?"

(1 point)

He asked her why she did not spend a few days with Alice.


8. 

Sheila asked Kate, "Have you got married?"

(1 point)

Sheila asked Kate if they had got married.


9. 

Lucy asked Jo, "Why wasn't the doctor sent for?"

(1 point)

Lucy asked Jo why the doctor hadn't been sent for.


10. 

He said to Bert, "When was all this decided?"

(1 point)

He asked Bert when all that had been decided.
