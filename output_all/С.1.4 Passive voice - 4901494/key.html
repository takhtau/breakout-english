С.1.4 Passive voice

Заполните пропуски, чтобы они грамматически соответствовали содержанию предложения. При отрицании, используйте сокращенную форму (don't, haven't, won't).


1. 

When_______the calendar_____________? (INVENT)

(1 point)

was/invented


2. 

The potatoes must____________. (PEEL)

(1 point)

be peeled


3. 

The potatoes_______________an hour ago. (PEEL)

(1 point)

were peeled


4. 

When_________the wallet_________? (LOSE)

(1 point)

was/lost


5. 

The city is beautiful now. All the squares_____________with Christmas trees. (DECORATE) 

(1 point)

are decorated


6. 

All the squares must________________ . (DECORATE) 

(1 point)

be decorated


7. 

The clothes________________yesterday. (PACK)

(1 point)

were packed


8. 

The clothes____________tomorrow. (PACK)

(1 point)

will be packed


9. 

The new words must_______________ . (COPY) 

(1 point)

be copied


10. 

The picture____________about 350 years ago. But it____________in the twentieth century. (PAINT/LOSE)

(1 point)

was painted/was lost
