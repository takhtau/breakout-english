C.1.7 Косвенная речь

Заполните пропуски, чтобы они грамматически соответствовали содержанию предложения. При отрицании, используйте сокращенную форму (don't, haven't, won't).


1. 

She said they________already________up the tent. (PUT) 

(1 point)

had/put


2. 

She said the boys____________up the tent an hour later. (PUT) 

(1 point)

would put


3. 

She said she_________already_________the door. (LOCK)

(1 point)

had/locked


4. 

She said she____________the door an hour door. (LOCK) 

(1 point)

would lock


5. 

The clothes must__________ . (WASH) 

(1 point)

be washed


6. 

The clothes_______________an hour ago. (WASH) 

(1 point)

were washed


7. 

She_________________the clothes yet. (NOT WASH) 

(1 point)

hasn't washed


8. 

She said she______________the clothes already. (WASH) 

(1 point)

had washed


9. 

She said she______________the clothes the next day. (WASH)

(1 point)

would wash


10. 

He said he______________the documents an hour later. (EXAMINE) 

(1 point)

would examine
